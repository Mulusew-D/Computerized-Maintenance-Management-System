About the System

This system helps hospitals and clinics manage maintenance of medical equipment in a structured and transparent way. Features include:

  • Equipment details: manufacturer, model, class, purchase date, location
  • Regulatory compliance tracking: calibration, safety checks
  • Preventive maintenance scheduling
  • Work order history and logs
  • Inventory management of spare parts
  • Reporting and analytics

This project is designed to help maintenance teams reduce downtime, improve safety, meet regulations, and improve the lifespan of medical devices.
